About ISB

The Image Security Barrier (ISB) is a proposed project that aims to tackle the issue of data leakage and privacy concerns when sharing images containing sensitive information on social media. By recognizing and hiding or encrypting sensitive data within images, ISB provides a practical and efficient solution to mitigate the risks associated with data breaches. The project's methodology involves using optical character recognition (OCR), keyword detection, pattern detection, and handling of QR codes and barcodes. Through implementation in Python and the utilization of various libraries, ISB achieves a high accuracy rate of 95.6% in securing sensitive information within sample images. The social impact of ISB is significant as it contributes to data privacy protection, instilling peace of mind for countless internet users. The application areas of ISB encompass social media platforms, commercial transactions, cloud services, and general data protection, making it a versatile tool for safeguarding sensitive data in various contexts.
